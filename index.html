Recyclable or Not

In this website, it will tell you what trash items are recyclable or on recyclable! If the classifyer classifys something as either carboard, paper, glass, plastic, or metal, the item is recyclable. If it considers it as trash, throw it in the trash!

About the Creators

The purpose of this website is to prevent people from placing items that could be recyclable if people knew that you have to clean certain products before you recycle them, or that you need to recycle items seperatly if it's something like a plastic container. Since a lot of people aren't aware of these recycling rules, only 5-6% of items taht are recyled each year are actually able to be recycled.

More Info!

Why is recycling important

Recycling is important because it reduces the amount of waste that ends up in landfills. It also reduces the amount of energy and resources to make new products.

Facts

  • Each ton (2,000 pounds) of recycled paper can save 17 trees

  • Plastic waste can take anywhere from 20 to 500 years to decompose, but it never really goes away

  • Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a 55-inch HDTV to watch your favorite movie
